Advice & Seasonal Notes

Spring

Pinching Pine Candles

For Japanese black pine in NSW, start pinching when candles extend about 2–3 cm.

Summer

Heatwave Protection

Provide afternoon shade cloth during extreme heat. Avoid pruning during multi-day heat events.

All Year

Substrate Basics

Free-draining mixes prevent root rot. Start with akadama, pumice, and lava; adjust per species.
